Use this form to delete answers submitted to assessments in this course, so that they can be re-submitted by students.

Student progress may be affected by this action, but any students who have already achieved their assessment milestone or passed the course will not lose these achievements.

Optionally select an individual student and/or assessment to delete only the answers submitted by that student / to that assessment.
